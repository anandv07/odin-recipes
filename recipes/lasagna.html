Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a mouthwatering Italian dish that combines layers of wide, flat pasta sheets with a rich and flavorful filling. Each component of lasagna comes together to create a symphony of taste and texture.The dish is assembled by alternating layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese, forming a delectable tower that is then baked until golden and bubbling.

With its comforting warmth and indulgent richness, lasagna is a classic crowd-pleaser that never fails to satisfy even the most discerning palate.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Lasagna sheets/noodles
  • Meat sauce
  • Cheese filling
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Additional Herbs

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles and drain them to prevent sticking.
  2. Prepare meat sauce by allowing the flavours to meld together.
  3. Prepare a creamy and flavorful cheese filling.
  4. Bake the Lasagna until the cheese is melted and golden brown.
