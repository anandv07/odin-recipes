Chicken Biriyani

Description

Chicken biryani is a delicious and aromatic rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent and has gained popularity worldwide. It is a complete meal in itself, combining tender chicken pieces with fragrant basmati rice and a blend of aromatic spices.

The dish typically includes ingredients such as onions, ginger, garlic, tomatoes, yogurt, and various spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Basmati rice
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and chilli powder
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken.
  2. Partially boil the rice.
  3. Add the ingredients and cook for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Layer the partially cooked rice over the cooked chicken. Spread it evenly.
  5. Cook over low heat for about 20-30 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked and tender.
